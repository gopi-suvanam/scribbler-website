Our Story

AI should run where
your data lives.

Scribbler is an open-source platform that runs LLMs, image generation, machine learning, and scientific computation entirely in your browser — using WebGPU and JavaScript. No server. No install. No data leaves your machine.

Our Mission

Democratize AI by making it
run on every device with a browser.

We give developers, researchers, and students the tools to build and share AI applications without cloud dependencies:

50+ Ready-to-Run Notebooks

LLM chat, Stable Diffusion, TensorFlow.js, Monte Carlo simulations, data visualization — one click to run.

Every Major AI Library

WebLLM, TensorFlow.js, ONNX Runtime, Transformers.js, Plotly, D3 — loaded dynamically from CDNs.

Deep Learning Paths

Curated articles and tutorials on AI, scientific computing, image processing, and data science.

Open Source & Self-Hostable

MIT licensed. Pure static files. Host on any server, S3 bucket, or air-gapped network.

Why this matters.

Privacy

Your prompts, images, and data stay on your device. No logs. No training on your inputs. This isn't a feature — it's the architecture.

Access

A student in Lagos and a researcher at MIT should have the same AI tools. Browser-based AI removes the server cost barrier.

Speed

No round-trip to the cloud. No cold starts. No rate limits. WebGPU puts your local GPU to work — results in milliseconds.

Sharing

Every notebook is a single URL. Share an AI experiment and they can run it immediately — no setup, no Docker, no dependency hell.
